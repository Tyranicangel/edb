Objectives of EDB

  1. To attract a large volume of investments in Andhra Pradesh and to develop it as the best destination for investments.
  2. To promote Ease of Doing Business in Andhra Pradesh.
  3. To enhance opportunities for wealth creation for the investors in the state.
  4. To develop strategies for large scale employment generation in AP.
  5. To promote strategies for accelerated economic development of the state.

TRUST IS KEY FOR ANY BUSINESS OPPORTUNITY IN AP

Businesses looking to set up in AP can expect integrity, adaptability and respect for intellectual capital to be emphasised upon by a strategic-minded administration, which is attuned to the needs of companies to protect invention and innovation.
